About us

Pr-care Institute Limited ("PR-CARE") is one of India’s largest healthcare organizations. We operate 17 healthcare facilities (3400+ beds) across the Mumbai, Haryana, Punjab, Uttarakhand and Maharashtra. Almost 85% of our bed capacity is in Metro/Tier 1 cities. Apart from hospitals, pr-care also operates a homecare business and pathology business under brand names PR@Home and PR Labs respectively. PR@Home offers health and wellness services at home while PR Lab provides Pathology Services outside our hospital network.
